The Mardan Model School & College Mardan(MMS&C) is a place where teachers, students and parents work together the whole year in the educational and extra-curricular activities . The MMS&C conducts several events like International Day, Sports day and the Arts and Crafts Exhibitions, to foster cognitive, innovative and leadership traits among the students. The School also arranges a number of curricular, co-curricular and cultural activities with in and out of the school premises, to expose its students to the contemporary challenges and opportunities. Educational trips during the year are a great source of information, inspiration and recreation to the students. Frequent parent teachers meetings are also conducted to ensure and update the parents about the welfare and positive grooming of their children.

The School provides all the facilities for professional development of its teachers through reputed international organizations to make them well equipped for the proper grooming and upbringing of the students at all levels. The MMS&C staff is also proactive in cultivating a positive attitude among the students so that they become reasonable and responsible citizens in the community in their practical lives.”
